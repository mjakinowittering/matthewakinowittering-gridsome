Acorn-i is a finalist for Amazon Ads Partner Awards 2021

2021-12-14

I am thrilled that Acorn Intelligence is a finalist for not one but two categories for the inaugural Amazon Ads Partner Awards!

Congratulations to our 2021 Amazon Ads Partner Awards' UK finalists: Amerge, @acorni1, Vyper Global, @ceutagroup, @marketplaceamp, @nozzle_ai, @MediaComUK, @Pacvue, & @mindshare_uk. https://t.co/g8QLK1hfqg pic.twitter.com/DNb5jnsQeq

We reached the final three in Innovation Award and Scaled Technology Award categories. As the Lead Product Manager for Ignite by Acorn-i, it is incredible that we've got to the last three for the Scaled Technology awards, along with Amerge and Pacvue.

The Scaled Technology award honours a trailblazing Amazon Ads partner who is integrated with the Amazon Ads API and has developed a technology application that has helped their UK clients expand their business to new regions over the past year. In addition, finalists have effectively leveraged Amazon Ads products to drive global growth for their clients.
